Our Heritage

Forging the Future Since 1982

CORE began as a small machine shop with a singular focus on precision. Today, we operate a global network of smart facilities, providing end-to-end manufacturing solutions for the world's most demanding industries.

Our commitment remains unchanged: uncompromising quality, relentless innovation, and a dedication to powering human progress through superior industrial engineering.

Our Mission

To deliver absolute precision at scale. We partner with innovators to transform complex blueprints into flawless physical realities, driving progress in aerospace, medical, and energy sectors.

Our Vision

To build the definitive smart-manufacturing ecosystem where human expertise and AI-driven robotics converge, setting the global standard for industrial quality and sustainable production.
