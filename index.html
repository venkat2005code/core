Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What industries do you specialize in?

We specialize exclusively in high-precision manufacturing for the aerospace, defense, and space exploration sectors, producing mission-critical components.

Are your facilities ISO certified?

Yes, our facilities are AS9100D (Aerospace) and ISO 9001:2015 certified, adhering to the strictest quality standards in the industry.

What is your typical lead time for custom aerospace projects?

Lead times vary depending on complexity and materials. Rapid prototyping of titanium parts can take as little as 5-10 days, while full-scale production runs are scheduled with our supply chain managers.
